Symptoms and fault codes

Common signs of a DQ200 TCM no communication fault.

These are the symptoms customers usually report before sending the module in for testing.

Typical symptoms

  • No communication with transmission control module
  • Gearbox warning light or transmission warning message
  • PRNDS flashing on the dashboard
  • Gear indicator missing or blank
  • Vehicle may not start or may lose drive
  • Diagnostic tools cannot detect the TCM
  • Intermittent communication that later becomes complete silence
