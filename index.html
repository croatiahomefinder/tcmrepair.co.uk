Symptoms and fault codes

Common signs of a DQ200 no communication fault.

Typical symptoms, dashboard behaviour and fault codes associated with this problem.

Typical symptoms

  • No communication with TCM
  • No start
  • Loss of drive
  • Gearbox or transmission warning lights
  • Fault messages on the dash
  • Intermittent or complete controller silence
  • PRNDS flashing on the dashboard
  • Gear indicator missing or blank
  • Diagnostic tools cannot detect the TCM
  • Communication faults stored alongside module faults

Common fault codes

U0101

Lost Communication with TCM

P1604

Internal Control Module Malfunction

P0562

System Voltage Low
