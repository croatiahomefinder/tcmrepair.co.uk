Shipping instructions

This part needs to be crystal clear. Confused customers do not convert. Worse, they post things like drunken raccoons.

Before sending your TCM

Remove the transmission control module from the mechatronic unit
Clean the module before packing
Wrap it properly in bubble wrap
Use a strong box with good padding
Include your contact details inside the parcel
Include vehicle details and a short fault description
Add any DTCs if available
Include a prepaid return label inside the box

Shipping responsibility

You choose the courier used to send the unit to us
You are responsible for arranging outbound shipping
You are also responsible for the prepaid return label
Please pack the unit properly to reduce transit risk
Keep your courier tracking details until the job is complete
We do not accept liability for units lost, damaged, delayed or destroyed in transit by any courier service.

Eligibility notes

Units that have been previously opened, tampered with or repaired elsewhere must be declared before shipping. Acceptance remains subject to inspection, and some units may be refused if prior work or damage affects repair viability.

Questions customers usually ask before booking

This mirrors the walnut site logic: answer the practical objections early, before they become inbox gravel.

What does the £149 include?

The standard service includes testing, inspection, repair of confirmed no communication faults, verification and a repair outcome report.

What if the unit cannot be repaired?

If the unit is confirmed as non-repairable under the terms of the service, the no repair, no fee policy applies to the repair charge.

What is the same-day option?

The same-day option adds priority handling on arrival and is intended for customers who want the fastest possible turnaround.

What is the preventative reliability package?

It is an optional add-on that addresses common factory weak points to help improve longer-term reliability.

Do you accept previously repaired units?

Not always. Any previous repair attempt, tampering or physical damage must be declared before shipping and remains subject to inspection.

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes. This is an appointment-only service. Please contact the workshop before travelling or sending anything blindly.
