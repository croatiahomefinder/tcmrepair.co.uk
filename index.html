Symptoms and fault codes

Common symptoms and DTCs for this DQ200 repair.

These are the common warning signs and diagnostic codes seen on DQ200 TCU no communication jobs.

Common symptoms we fix

  • No communication with TCU or cannot connect via diagnostics
  • Intermittent comms, PRNDS flashing, limp mode or no drive
  • 30A mechatronic fuse blowing when the TCU is connected
  • Other modules log TCM offline while the TCU itself is silent
  • Gear indicator missing, blank or gearbox warning displayed
  • Sporadic drop-outs on power, ground or CAN related to the TCU

Typical DTCs related to this repair

U0101

Lost Communication with TCM

01315

Transmission Control Module: No Signal / Communication

P0606 / P0607

Internal control module processor / performance fault

P1602

Power supply B+ low (terminal 30)

P0562 / 00532

System voltage low / supply voltage

Before you book - quick checks

  • Verify the 30A mechatronic fuse, battery health and main grounds first.
  • If other modules log U0101 or 01315 and the TCU is silent, this service is usually appropriate.
  • If the main fault is P189C or P17BF relating to pressure or accumulator issues, message us first.
  • If you only have odd or even gear drive faults, that is usually a different fault path.

FAQ

Questions customers ask before sending a unit.

Straight answers before you send your unit.

Does this fix no comms?

Yes. This service is for DQ200 TCU no communication faults where the unit is silent or cannot be reached by diagnostics. We restore the communication path and verify the result on the bench.

Do I need coding afterwards?

Refitting the original TCU usually requires no coding. Clear the faults and perform basic DSG adaptations if prompted by the vehicle.

What is the turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is usually 1 to 2 working days from receipt. Express aims for same-day or next-day handling where the parcel arrives before 11am and the unit is suitable for immediate work.

What if the unit is not repairable?

If there is heavy corrosion, severe PCB damage or another unrelated fault path, we will advise. The diagnostic-only fee is £45 and is waived if the unit is repaired.

Can I bring the complete vehicle?

Yes, by appointment. On-car remove, repair and refit work starts from £449 plus oil or gaskets where required.

What should I put in the parcel?

Include your name, phone number, return address, REG/VIN, part number and a pre-paid return label inside the box. Add fault codes if you have them.
